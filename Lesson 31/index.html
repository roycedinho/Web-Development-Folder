About Codingal

A little bit about me

Codingal is on a mission to inspire school kids to fall in love with coding. Coding helps develop logical thinking and problem-solving skills. Coding jobs are the future. They already constitute more than 60% of all jobs in STEM. Starting young means students will be ahead by college. They will be the creators of the future.

In today’s age, coding has become an essential skill. It helps kids interact better with technology. It aids holistic development—boosting cognitive, physical, and social skills. Coding encourages both mental growth and social understanding.
