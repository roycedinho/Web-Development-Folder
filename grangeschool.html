Grange School

Introduction

Grange School is a leading educational institution located in Lagos, Nigeria. It provides a high-quality education through the British International Curriculum.

School Details

Address: Harold Shodipo Crescent, GRA Ikeja, Lagos, Nigeria

Total Students Enrolled: Approximately 1,200

Streams Offered:

Science (with Physics1, Chemistry2, and Biology3)
Arts (including Literature4 and History5)
Commerce (Accounts6 and Economics7)

Educational Board

The school is affiliated with the British International Curriculum and focuses on nurturing students to excel globally.

Fun Fact

Grange School has been providing excellence in education for over 60 years*.
